Purpose

What we want to share

This page is not a list of activities. It is a narrative of communion: a local Verbum Dei family that wants to keep listening to the Word, to people, and to the signs of the times.

1

Who we are

A local expression of the Missionary Family Verbum Dei in Lisbon.

2

How we serve

Through groups, initiatives, coordination bodies and shared apostolic discernment.

3

What we need

Clearer co-responsibility, formation, sustainability and missionary focus.

Identity

A local family within the global Verbum Dei Family

The Missionary Family Verbum Dei of Lisbon belongs to the wider Verbum Dei Family and shares its spirituality, mission and service to the Word of God.

Belonging

Different states of life

People and groups from different states of life, ages, cultures and social conditions participate in the same spirituality and mission.

Local expression

Lisbon community

Lisbon is formed through the presence of FMVD missionaries, missionary couples, committed missionary disciples and people who feel part of the FaMVD.

Common mission

Charism in communion

To live and serve the charism in communion with the wider Verbum Dei Family and the Church.

Apostolic life

What we do and how we do it

The community has grown through initiative, availability and autonomy. Groups have emerged as responses to what the Spirit inspired.

Youth

Coordination and accompaniment of Verbum Dei youth groups.

Mission

Activities that foster the missionary dynamism of the community.

Couples & families

Support for dating, marriage, parenting and family life.

Reviews & deepening

Spaces for revision, reflection and spiritual deepening.

Support areas

Other confirmed areas include Fraternal Funds, Communication and Ré Maior / ecology and sustainability.

Organisation

Coordination as fraternal service

Coordination is understood as a fraternal service lived in function of the charism, seeking communion, co-responsibility and representativeness.

Community Assembly

Open forum for committed participants; debates, clarifies and evaluates matters concerning the whole FaMVD Lisboa.

Representative Apostolic Council

Shares the journey, challenges and needs of each group; proposes and evaluates initiatives for the common apostolic project.

Coordinating Group

Permanent and regular coordination body, proposing direction, supporting groups and facilitating community life.

Reading the community

What has been done and what needs attention

The documents point to a process of planning and organisation, while also naming the need for clearer coordination, communication and collaboration between groups.

What still needs community confirmation

Next stage

From autonomy to co-responsibility

The next step is not to reduce initiative, but to make initiative more connected, sustainable and missionary.

Direction

A common direction that helps the community converge without losing the creativity of each group.

Participation

A broader sense of belonging, where more people feel invited to carry the community.

Support

Clearer accompaniment of groups, transition folders, shared planning and better internal communication.

Missionary focus

Read the signs of the times, understand what people are seeking, and offer accessible paths of encounter, community and mission.
